About

Find Out More About Us

At Vetlife Innovations, we specialize in advanced feed supplements that support the health, growth, and productivity of Livestock, Poultry, and Aquaculture animals.

Our focus is on scientifically formulated nutritional solutions that help strengthen immunity, enhance growth, and optimize farm performance.


Livestock Health – We provide feed supplements for cattle, sheep, goats, and other farm animals. Our solutions enhance immunity, promote growth, support digestion, and improve productivity, leading to better milk yield, meat quality, and farm efficiency.


Poultry Solutions – Our feed additives and supplements boost growth, improve egg production, and enhance disease resistance in broilers, layers, and breeders, with a focus on gut health, immunity, and overall performance.


Aquaculture Care – We offer specialized feed supplements for fish and shrimp farming, addressing nutritional gaps, improving feed conversion, and promoting healthier, faster-growing aquatic stock.
